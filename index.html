About Us

Fiction Entertainment is a visionary creative, production and advertising agency based in Nairobi, Kenya. The full-service creative agency, formed in 2012 to bring together strategic design and intelligent thinking, has since been the source of inspiration and creativity working for the branding, re-branding and general communication of several national and international brands.

Since 2017, Fiction Entertainment celebrated its 5th year in business by setting up a studio where like-minded and passionate people can work together under one roof, specializing in developing honest advertising and branding projects, making ideas more insightful and effective, sharing unique ideas, and bring them to life. When we say We are Fiction It’s all because we deal with the creation of visual brand strategy, brand positioning, and project implementation in the field of corporate and consumer identity. Focusing on: simplicity, strategy, functionality, dialogue with clients, and hard work in order to create a strong, lasting, and remarkable design, brand, message, or campaign. Simple and personal.

Our process helps us create head-turning brands, but it means nothing if those turning heads aren’t moved to action that drives results. We work with businesses and organizations of all shapes and sizes, from early-stage startups to big brands, to create products and services —print and digital— that solve the problems of today as well as define the opportunities of the future.

Fiction Entertainment makes everything needed to capture peoples’ hearts and minds. To compel them to call, click, comment, share and buy. Fiction Entertainment considers every project as unique, over the last Decade we’ve grown, always having a single component at its heart: a passionate idea and how to make the intangible, tangible.
